ABOUT ME

Let me make your dream a reality

Photography is a profession for some. For me - photography is a way of sketching my dreams and making them perceptible. I love what I do and my intent is to share this love through the images that I create. My photography style can be described as creative, photojournalistic and relaxed.

Love is the gravitational force that attracts two beings towards each other. When two people in love decide to become one - it entails a lot of emotion, drama, excitement, madness, dancing and sometimes, drinking too! It gives me an opportunity to capture all of these elements and document them in a way that they become a timeless piece of beauty for the couple to cherish for a lifetime.

I have been shooting in the Bay Area - covering all kind of events and weddings; and even volunteered if the opportunity is something that I love. San Francisco Bay Area is home to a plentitude of cultures and being a photographer has given me the opportunity to experience the rituals and customs that imbue within these myriad cultures. I've had the opportunity to shoot American, Persian and Indian weddings, Jewish Bar Mitzvah's, Christian Baptisms, Baby Showers, Half Saree, Birthdays, Engagements and many more diverse events.

As you look through my portfolio, I hope that you are able to find elements of style that match your taste and can appreciate the composition and the use of natural light. If you are inspired by what you just saw - do not hesitate to get in touch with me and ask any questions that you may have.
